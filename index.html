H.U.G. Reading Program

Help Us Grow

The Help Us Grow Reading Program was created with the objective of helping kids with a lower than average reading level. It was designed to host weekly reading lessons, meeting the needs of individual students. H.U.G. is nearing the end of it's development phase, and is planning to deploy in Louisville, Ky in the near future.

Weatherly

Weatherly was an older project designed for the user to enter either a zip or city/state, and receive the current weather conditions in that area. This is accomplished using Dark Sky API.

Tip Calculator

This is a basic tip calculator used to determine how much of a tip to leave your server. The calculator receives user inputs including bill amount, quality of service, and how many people are sharing the bill.

Flash Cards

Learn how to add.

Flash Cards is a simple app developed using Angular 2. It shows a card with a random addition problem, and at the touch of a button, shows the answer to that problem. Future versions of this app will include difficulty levels and subtraction, multiplication, and division problems.
